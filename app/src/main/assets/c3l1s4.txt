...and new rights<<-->>Among the rights for citizens introduced by the GDPR are right of access (Article 15: the citizen must be able to know immediately how and why they are processing his data), the right of rectification (Article 16: «correction of inaccurate personal data concerning him »), the right to oblivion (Article 17: the user can have his data deleted) and the right to data portability (Article 20: the user can receive all the information concerning him and forward them to another holder).
